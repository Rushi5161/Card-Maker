The Great Indian Kapil Show

2024 - 2026 TV-14 4 Seasons Ultra HD 4K

India's most famous comedian, Kapil Sharma, moves his celebrated talk show to Netflix for a global audience. Set in a fictional airport terminal, the series features the historic reunion of Kapil and Sunil Grover. The show blends celebrity interviews, sketch comedy, and stand-up, featuring fan-favorite characters and a star-studded guest list from Bollywood and beyond.

Starring: Kapil Sharma, Sunil Grover, Krushna Abhishek, Kiku Sharda, Archana Puran Singh.

Genre: Hindi, Comedy, Talk Shows & Variety Entertainment.

This Show is: Hilarious, Heartfelt, Nostalgic.
