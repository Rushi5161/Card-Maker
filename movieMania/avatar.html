The Third Chapter

Avatar: Fire and Ash

Released: Dec 2025 UA 16+ 3h 17m Sci-Fi / Epic

The Sully family faces their darkest trial yet. In the aftermath of the battle for the oceans, Jake and Neytiri encounter the Mangkwan Clan—also known as the "Ash People." Led by the ruthless Varang, this volcanic tribe has rejected the ways of Eywa and allied with the RDA, bringing a new, violent philosophy to Pandora that challenges everything the Na'vi stand for.

Starring: Sam Worthington, Zoe Saldaña, Sigourney Weaver, Oona Chaplin

Director: James Cameron
